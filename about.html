Jake Allyne - President & CEO

Jake Allyne is a fitness professional based in Westchester, NY and the surrounding areas. Working in the industry for the last 9 years, Jake specializes in exercise programming to establish proper biomechanics and correct movement patterns within a wide variety of goals ranging from athletes, to the daily mover and corrective exercise. Jake also has a great deal of experience with the special needs community and it is his passion to work with them. He wasn't the most in shape or athletic kid growing up. Having overcome his own struggle of self confidence and fitness, he can connect and understand what we may or may not be going through in our fitness journey. Personally, he has competed in many events from marathons in mountains to physique bodybuilding. Additionally, he has worked with the NY Giants and athletes alike. He continues to learn and explore because he believes in practicing what he preaches. His mission is to share his passion; that passion is simply seeing you achieve your goal, big or small, because you are capable of so much more than you know. "If you can dream it, you can achieve it."

Stephen Goodman - CFO & COO

Stephen has had a long career in healthcare, working at Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center, focusing on optimizing operational workflows and rolling out new institutional initiatives. His personal fitness journey started in 2009, when he first started weightlifting at his high school. It wasn't long before he was trying to get in there whenever he could to keep improving day after day. This led him to pursue an undergraduate degree from SUNY Cortland in Kinesiology, the study of movement. Nearing completion of his undergraduate program, Stephen found a new passion, business and finance. This led him to complete his MBA at Mercy College, concentrating in Finance and graduating Summa Cum Laude. Stephen is a life-long resident of Westchester, and in his free time, enjoys exploring the beautiful nature the county has to offer, from hiking Bear Mountain, to the scenic Riverwalk in Tarrytown. Stephen is also involved with the Special Needs community in the area and is a head coach for the Sound Shore Stars Special Olympics Team based in Larchmont, NY.
