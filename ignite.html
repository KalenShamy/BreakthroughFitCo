What is IGNITE?

Ignite offers progressive, fun, and adaptive fitness programs to members of the Westchester special needs community, ages 12 through adulthood.

Through IGNITE's fitness assessment process, members are aligned based on their abilities, to ensure a structured and nurturing environment.

IGNITE serves a broad spectrum of disabilities and aims to empower each individual through exercise and positive experiences, enhancing social skills, and making new friends within our community.

Our Mission

We exist to create equal opportunity to health and fitness, enhancing the lives of those with developmental disabilities through a high-quality, progressive, community-based fitness program. We aim to create fun and engaging moments, creating a positive impact on the lives of each individual.
