Are you ready to Breakthrough?

Breakthrough Fit Co is a personal training and small group training facility that exists to adapt to individuals life styles through group, in person, and virtual training options.


More importantly we exist to create equal access to health and wellness for the special needs community through our adaptive and personalized IGNITE Fitness Program.


Exercise and a balanced lifestyle is the first line of defense against an array of things that life can throw at you. Here at Breakthrough, we want you to not only achieve your goals, but the quality of life that we believe every individual deserves.
